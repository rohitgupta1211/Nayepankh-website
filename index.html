About Us

Empowering Youth, Transforming Communities

NayePankh Foundation is a student-led NGO dedicated to empowering youth through education, skill development, social awareness, and community impact initiatives across India.

We believe every student deserves opportunities to learn, grow, and contribute meaningfully to society.
